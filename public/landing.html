About Call a Nanny

We provide trusted, professional childcare services across Marrakech. Whether you're staying at a hotel, riad, or private villa, our verified nannies come to you.

Every nanny undergoes thorough background checks, in-person interviews, and holds certifications in childcare and first aid. We match your family with caregivers who speak your language and understand your needs.
